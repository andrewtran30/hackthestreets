madHacks 2020 Registration

The form for the official registration for madHacks 2020 is to the right of the screen. Official registration ends on January 10, 2020. On site registration may be a possibility, however, it is not guarenteed that you will be able to participate. By registering to madHacks 2020, you are making a commitment that you will show up and participate on March 14-15.

Connecting with our sponsors

If you are a member of an underrepresented community, then you are eligible to upload your resumé by clicking the button below. Your uploaded resumé will be reviewed by the hackathon executive directors and sent to certain sponsors for paid/unpaid mentorship, internship, or other prosepctive opportunities. Please know that while uploading a resumé puts you in a strong position for a future opportunity, it does not guarentee one.
