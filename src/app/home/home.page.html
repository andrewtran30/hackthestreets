Learn. Code. Create.

Have you ever wanted to build autonomous drones, amazing iOS/Android apps, cool games, or experiment with artificial intelligence? Whether you're an experienced developer, or just starting to learn how to program, there's a place for you at HackTheStreets. In the 24 hours at HackTheStreets, you can attend workshops, meet new people, and develop amazing applications. With that being said, get ready for an adventure at HackTheStreets 2020!

IMPORTANT: If you are a minority or a member of an underrepresented community, you are eligible to connect with our sponsors for a mentorship/internship opportunity. Visit the registration page for more information.
