Learn. Code. Create.

Have you ever wanted to build autonomous drones, amazing iOS/Android apps, cool games, or experiment with artificial intelligence? Whether you're an experienced developer, or just starting to learn how to program, there's a place for you at madHacks. In the 24 hours at madHacks, you can attend workshops, meet new people, and develop amazing applications. With that being said, get ready for an adventure at madHacks 2020!
